F1 Grand Prix Lap Time Manager

An Interactive Project in DSA By: Cope, So, Palmiano, Nebria
Hello there! Welcome to our F1 Grand Prix Lap Time Manager. This web app is designed to help you manage your lap times in a Formula 1 Grand Prix. You can add, search, and delete lap times. You can also view the lap times in different orders, this to ensure that you are always on top of your game!
